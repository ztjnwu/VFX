Don't get COT!

Hello everyone, we are Chaitra Boggaram, Oluwabusayo Omotosho, and Tengjiao Zhu, and we are graduate students at Santa Clara University. Today, we are excited to present to you COT, a state-of-the-art plagiarism checker that is designed to help students and professionals ensure that their work is original and free from any potential instances of plagiarism.



As graduate students ourselves, we understand the importance of academic integrity and the potential consequences of plagiarism. That's why we have worked with the team at COT to develop a tool that is both accurate and user-friendly, making it easy for anyone to check their work for plagiarism before submitting it for grading or publication.



COT uses advanced algorithms and machine learning techniques to compare the input file to files generated by ChatGPT, a state-of-the-art language model developed by OpenAI. The tool then highlights any matching text in the input file and calculates the plagiarism percentage based on the amount of matching text found.



We believe that COT is a valuable resource for anyone concerned about maintaining academic and professional integrity in their work. Whether you are a student, a researcher, or a professional, COT can help you ensure that your work is original and properly sourced.



Thank you for your attention, and we hope that you find COT to be a helpful tool in your academic and professional pursuits.
